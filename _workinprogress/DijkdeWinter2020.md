title: "Monitoring the business cycle and nowcasting GDP by textmining financial news"

venue: 'Mimeo'

Recent research indicates the potential usefulness of using text as data. This research aims to explore the possibilities of using the 1985-2018 archive of the only financial Dutch newspaper to monitor the Dutch business cycle and nowcast GDP growth. We compare the outcomes of text-based nowcasts using a tone adjusted Bayesian mixture model with the short-term forecasts of dynamic factor models. We contribute to the existing literature on topic models by introducing real-time estimation and hierarchy in the topic model structure.

Joint with Dorinth van Dijk (De Nederlandsche Bank and MIT Center for Real Estate).
