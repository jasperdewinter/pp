Recent years have been marked by the recovery of the crisis. Although this process is not yet fully completed, there is also room to look ahead. What is the current state of the Dutch economy? How can we earn our money in the future? And what can we do to strengthen growth? This study chooses a long-term perspective on growth by examining how global trends change the character of the Dutch economy. This image, combined with an analysis of the sources of labor productivity growth, provides concrete leads for policy that promotes growth.
